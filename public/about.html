About Me

I was born in Edo state Nigeria on 14 July 1987.
I finished my primary education in 1999 and my secondary education in 2005. I studied at the University of Benin where I obtained a degree in Health Education in 2009.
I obtained a master’s degree in Counselling Psychology in 2016.

I have worked in Federal government girl’s college Benin under the Federal Ministry of Education as a basic science teacher since 2014, as a counsellor since 2017, STEAM club patroness and coderina coach for Lego league championship since 2021.
